Projects

  1. UTILIZING MACHINE LEARNING FOR FACE MASK DETECTION.

    • The project's main goal is to follow the WHO's recommendations to stop the spread of Covid-19 and to compare the accuracy of various machine learning algorithms. Pre-processing, data augmentation, training, testing, and image segmentation are some of the processes that go into accomplishing the project's goal. Following the procedures, a segmented image of the input dataset of individuals wearing masks and those not wearing them will be obtained with the aid of the Mask R-CNN algorithm. The model is then put into practice using a webcam to produce results for individuals wearing masks and those who are not, along with a percentage of accuracy. Creating a model that can identify individuals who are not wearing masks in public areas is the goal of this project.

    • Tools and Technologies: Raspberry pi 3B, Python.
